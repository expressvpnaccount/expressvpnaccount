How to Log In to Your ExpressVPN Account
----------------------------------------

Follow these steps to sign in to your ExpressVPN account:

1. Open your preferred web browser.
2. Visit the official ExpressVPN website.
3. Click the **Sign In** option.
4. Enter your registered email address.
5. Enter your account password.
6. Complete any verification step if prompted.
7. Click **Sign In** to access your account dashboard.

How to Reset Your ExpressVPN Password
-------------------------------------

If you cannot remember your password, follow these steps:

1. Go to the ExpressVPN sign-in page.
2. Select **Forgot Password**.
3. Enter the email address associated with your account.
4. Check your inbox for the password reset email.
5. Follow the instructions to create a new password.
6. Return to the login page and sign in using your updated credentials.

Common ExpressVPN Login Issues
------------------------------

Some common login issues include:

- Incorrect email address or password.
- Expired or inactive subscription.
- Browser cache or cookie conflicts.
- Internet connectivity problems.
- Temporary server or service interruptions.

Tips for Secure Account Access
------------------------------

For better account security:

- Use a strong, unique password.
- Do not share your login credentials.
- Sign out after using a shared or public computer.
- Keep your recovery email address up to date.
- Update your password regularly.
